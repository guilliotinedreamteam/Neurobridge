# Product Guide

## 1. Vision
NeuroBridge is a breakthrough Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) system designed to decode ECoG signals into speech in real-time. It bridges the gap between neural activity and communication with unprecedented speed and accuracy.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Researchers:** Neuroscientists studying speech decoding.
- **Clinicians:** Medical professionals working with locked-in patients.
- **Patients:** Individuals with speech impairments seeking a voice.

## 3. Core Features
- **Real-time Decoding:** <50ms latency from signal to phoneme.
- **High-Density Visualization:** 128-channel raw signal monitoring.
- **Adaptive Signal Health:** Auto-rejection of noisy channels.
- **Speech Synthesis:** Converting decoded phonemes to audio.
